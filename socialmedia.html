Our Social Media Team
Our social media team is the voice of RDC online. From creating posts that raise awareness about rare diseases to sharing updates about events and opportunities, this team helps spread our mission to a wider audience. Joining the social media team is a chance to build leadership, creativity, and communication skills while making an impact.
