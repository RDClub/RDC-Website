ABOUT US
At the Rare Disease Club, our mission is to raise awareness for conditions that are often overlooked, underfunded, and misunderstood. We work to educate students, create impactful advocacy projects, and support families and individuals affected by rare diseases.

Through campaigns, events, and community involvement, we aim to build a more informed and empathetic world for those navigating rare conditions.
