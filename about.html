About Us

Our Story

The Rare Disease Club was founded in 2020 with a mission to bring attention to the thousands of rare diseases that affect millions of people worldwide but receive minimal public awareness, research funding, and therapeutic development.

What started as a small campus initiative has grown into a nationwide movement with chapters at universities across the country, all working together to advocate for those affected by rare diseases.

Our Mission

To spread awareness about rare diseases, support affected individuals and families, advocate for policy changes, and fund research that leads to better treatments and cures.

Our Values

Compassion

We approach our work with empathy and understanding for those affected by rare diseases.

Advocacy

We are committed to being a voice for the rare disease community in all spaces.

Education

We believe knowledge is power and work to educate both the public and medical community.

Community

We build supportive networks that connect patients, families, researchers, and advocates.
