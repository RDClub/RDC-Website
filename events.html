Events & Fundraisers

2025

Falcon Fest 2025

For spooky season, we joined Falcon Fest 2025 with our jewelry booth — filled with fall spirit and creativity! Every purchase helped raise funds for RDC’s mission to support rare disease research and awareness.

San Jose Makers Market

Continuing our passion for creativity and advocacy, we sold our handmade jewelry at the San Jose Makers Market, connecting with new supporters and sharing our story with the local community.

Jewelry Design & Sales at The SoSo Market, Berkeley

RDC members showcased handcrafted jewelry at The SoSo Market in Berkeley, raising funds and awareness.

Chipotle Fundraiser for Oakland Children's Hospital

Our Chipotle fundraiser brought the community together — proceeds purchased toys and care packages for children at Oakland Children's Hospital.

Guest Speaking at Hart Middle School

We shared our mission at Hart Middle School to raise awareness about rare diseases and inspire compassion.

Bracelet Workshop for Falcon Fest

Members prepared handmade jewelry for Falcon Fest during this hands-on workshop.

Holiday Care Package & Blanket Drive

We assembled care packages and blankets for hospice patients and cancer support organizations.
