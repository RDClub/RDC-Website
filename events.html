WHAT WE DO

Rare Disease Collective (RDC) is a student-led initiative working to raise awareness about rare diseases. We aim to educate communities, support early diagnosis, and promote conversations around conditions that often go unnoticed due to limited research and public understanding.

Events & Fundraisers

2025
Falcon Fest 2025

Falcon Fest 2025

For spooky season, we joined Falcon Fest 2025 with our jewelry booth — filled with fall spirit and creativity! Every purchase helped raise funds for RDC’s ongoing mission to support rare disease research and awareness.

San Jose Makers Market

San Jose Makers Market

Continuing our passion for creativity and advocacy, we sold our handmade jewelry at the San Jose Makers Market, connecting with new supporters and sharing our story with the local community.

SoSo Market — Berkeley

Jewelry Design & Sales at The SoSo Market, Berkeley

RDC members designed and handcrafted high-quality jewelry to showcase and sell at The SoSo Market in Berkeley, raising funds to support our nonprofit mission.

Chipotle Fundraiser

Chipotle Fundraiser for Oakland Children's Hospital

Our Chipotle fundraiser brought the community together for a great cause! Proceeds went toward purchasing toys and care packages for children at Oakland Children's Hospital, spreading smiles and hope.

Guest Speaking

Guest Speaking at Hart Middle School

We kicked off 2025 by guest speaking at Hart Middle School, sharing our mission and raising awareness about rare diseases. It was an inspiring opportunity to educate students and spark compassion within the community.

2024
Bracelet Workshop

Bracelet Workshop for Falcon Fest

Members came together for a fun, hands-on bracelet-making workshop to prepare for Falcon Fest. Participants earned volunteer hours and helped raise funds for RDC’s cause.

Holiday Drive

Holiday Care Package & Blanket Drive

We ended the year by assembling care packages and cozy blankets for hospice patients and the Cancer Society — a heartfelt way to give back during the holiday season.

Speech and Debate Camp 2024

Speech and Debate Camp 2024

Our very first RDC event! The camp brought together passionate students to strengthen public speaking skills — all in support of our mission to raise $1,000 for Cure Rare Disease.
