🌟 Our Events & Impact
At the Rare Disease Club, we believe in turning passion into action. Here’s a look at some of the events and initiatives we’ve led to raise awareness, fund research, and support patients and families affected by rare diseases.
🦩 Summer Camp Fundraiser — July 2024
We hosted a summer camp that raised $1,000 for Cure Rare Disease. Young students participated in engaging activities while contributing to meaningful advocacy.
📚 Bracelet Workshop — September 2024
In preparation for Falcon Fest, members gathered to craft handmade bracelets, combining creativity and community spirit to support our upcoming fundraiser.
