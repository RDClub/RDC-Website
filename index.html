WHAT WE DO

Rare Disease Collective (RDC) is a student-led initiative working to raise awareness about rare diseases. We aim to educate communities, support early diagnosis, and promote conversations around conditions that often go unnoticed due to limited research and public understanding.

Monthly Events

SELLING AT JEWELRY MARKETS

We design and sell handmade jewelry at local markets to raise funds for rare disease research and awareness initiatives.

FUNDRAISERS

We host restaurant fundraisers, organize blanket drives, and donate care packages to support patients and families affected by rare diseases.

GUEST SPEAKING

We share our story and raise awareness by speaking at schools, community events, and online spaces, encouraging others to learn and get involved.
