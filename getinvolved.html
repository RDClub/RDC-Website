Every action helps us champion the 7,000 known rare diseases that impact millions of families. Explore the pathways below to find the opportunity that speaks to you.

Rare Disease Club is a student-led community dedicated to raising awareness, supporting early diagnosis, and uplifting families navigating rare conditions. Whether you have an hour a week or want to launch an entire chapter, there is a place for you here.

Volunteer With Us

Volunteers are the heartbeat of our outreach. From assembling awareness kits to designing social campaigns, you can match your talents with community needs.

  • Lead awareness workshops for classrooms and clubs.
  • Design graphics and stories that amplify rare disease voices.
  • Coordinate fundraisers that benefit local and national partners.

Launch a Chapter

Ready to lead? Start an RDC chapter to connect students, families, and healthcare professionals in your community.

  • Receive a starter toolkit with agendas, outreach templates, and event ideas.
  • Collaborate with our executive board for mentorship and partnership introductions.
  • Host quarterly programs that spotlight local rare disease stories.
Apply to Start a Chapter

Support Mission 7K

Mission 7K fuels scholarships, family care packages, and educational outreach for each of the 7,000 known rare diseases.

  • Every donation extends our educational resources and family support efforts.
  • Monthly gifts sustain year-round awareness campaigns and events.
  • Corporate sponsorships amplify impact through matching programs.
